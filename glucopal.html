GlucoPal

RGD Student Awards Winner for Accessibility Design

Branding | Illustration | Prototyping | Copywriting

GlucoPal is a health app that helps young adults who are newly diagnosed with diabetes manage they condition. App features enable them to stay on track with blood sugar, medication, appointments and boost their confidence in staying healthy. They know the app has their back, they may forget but GlucoPal won’t!

Group project with Carmen Huang (Branding, Illustration, Prototyping)
