Having a product that is an all encompassing guide to using a tattoo machine safely is a highly sought out design solution for new tattooists. This is because the kit will focus more on the education and safety aspect of the tattooing process with artful infographics to entice users to follow the instructions. A breakdown in machine cleaning, tips, sanitization, and other aspects of tattooing, will help drill in that safety and knowledge comes above the art.
