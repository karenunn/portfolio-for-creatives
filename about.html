  • Hæ. (that's Icelandic for hi)

    My name is Karen Unnsteinsdottir.

    My logo is a strawberry; there aren't many of them in Iceland where my family comes from. But there are plenty of poems, which we call sagas.

    There is also an ancient tradition—there in the land of snow rock and volcanos— of picking berries very patiently and carefully because there are so few of them. The tradition is known as Berjamó. It's a sensibility I bring to my design thinking and crafting.

    I am looking for work as a junior designer in a studio that values storytelling, design, and patiently picking strawberries.
