Georgia

Karen Unnsteinsdottir & Katarina Yaremkewich & Tin Raganit

Roles: Copy Writing, Branding, Illustration

GGeorgia was created with the mission to be an inclusive fashion brand that tackles UN Sustainable Development Goal 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production) by using skins from discarded peaches from the Okanagan as the main fibre in our product’s fabric production. Georgia undergarments are made for comfort, hygiene, and breathability, specifically accommodating vaginal discharge. Our brand is committed to normalizing vaginal discharge—  a daily experience for many women and people with vaginas. To achieve this, our brand’s copywriting encourages the audience to “Love your pulp” and “Take charge of your discharge”, carrying an empowered and unapologetic voice throughout our brand collateral. Peach imagery is abundant in our brand to honour our undergarments’ sustainable peach skin fibre. Our colour palette is representative of peaches and vaginal health. Our bold, eye-catching photography style maintains our brand colours and is reminiscent of peach fuzzes. Our illustration style, incorporated in our packaging, is cheeky and playful, incorporating imagery of the fruit and its blossoms. These components come together to create a strong and engaging social media presence.
