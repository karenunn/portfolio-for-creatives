Georgia

Art Direction, Social Media, Copy Writing

Georgia is a cheeky and sustainable underwear brand. Made with fabrics produced from discarded peach from BC Okhnangan fruit belt. Made for comfort, hygiene, and breathability, the undergarments are designed to destigmatize vaginal discharge. The playful tones encourages people to take charge of their discharge.

Group project with Katarina Yaremkewich (Branding) & Tin Raganit (Illustration, Packaging)

  • My Saga

    Hæ. (that's Icelandic for hi) My name is Karen Unnsteinsdottir, which means the daughter of waves crashing on rocks in Icelandic. Very poetic. My logo is a strawberry; there aren't many of them in Iceland where my family comes from. But there are plenty of poems, which we call sagas.

    There is also an ancient tradition-there in the land of snow rock and volcanos-, of picking berries very patiently and carefully because there are so few of them. The tradition is known as Berjamó. It's a sensibility I bring to my design thinking and crafting.

    I am about to graduate from the IDEA school of design at Capilano University in Spring of 2025. I am looking for work as a junior designer in a studio that values storytelling, design, and patiently picking strawberries.
